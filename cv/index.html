Projects

  • COAST: Duplexed RT-RPA SARS-CoV-2 Assay with Lateral Flow Readout
    • LOD: ~25 copies/reaction (fluorescence) and ~50 copies/reaction (LFA), ~15–28 min total runtime
    • Dual readout using TwistAmp exo kits with internally-quenched probes
    • Detects Delta and Omicron; no cross-reactivity with Flu A/B or common HCoVs
    • Demonstrated duplexed LFA with separate lines for target and process control
  • Chip-based ANSA for Quantitative HIV Viral-Load Testing
    • Systematically varied PEG to shrink site diameter and reduce merging
    • Expanded ANSA’s quantitative range to ~4.7 orders of magnitude
    • Automated image processing to count nucleation sites and extract Gaussian features
    • Demonstrated simple fabrication and 39 °C operation suitable for POC workflows
  • CNN-based Quantification of Isothermal Amplification (ANSA)
    • Defined temporal channel selection and preprocessing for model input
    • ResNet-18 architecture with hyperparameter optimization
    • Reported performance via clinical-utility and logarithmic-scale confusion matrices
    • Demonstrated data pipeline from raw frames → peak detection → model training
    • Extended the dnyamic range of the system of 6 orders of magnitude- rivals PCR
